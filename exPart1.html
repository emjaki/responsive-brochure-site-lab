PART 1 – Negative Press 20.11.2021 – 18.12.2021

The 2 screenprints on the South Wall were published posthumously and are part of a series of 6 screenprints made from 2017 onwards with the artist. While many plans were made for future series of works, these will be the only prints published posthumously by Negative Press. John had planned these out with full scale studies using coloured paper to indicate colour and composition. Published in pairs in 2017 and 2019, the screenprints illustrated here follow the same logic of combining a formal geometric composition along with a relaxed geometric arrangement.
